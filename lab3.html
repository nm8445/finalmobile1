Welcome to Lab 3

In my third lab, I made a multi-screen app using Android Studio to learn about handling multiple activities. I managed various input types and ensured smooth navigation between screens. The app, based on a topic of my choice, included two screens: a main screen and a functional screen for user interaction. I made a quiz app about cars. I incorporated five different input types, such as text fields, buttons, dropdown menus, and sliders, as well as one sensor or external data interaction like the camera or location services. The app design focused on a professional and personalized interface with custom colours, fonts, and images. After completing the app, I tested it on both an Android emulator and an external emulator, ensuring all functionalities worked seamlessly.
